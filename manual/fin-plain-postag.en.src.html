PoS-tag Finnish plaintext

The Finnish plaintext PoS-tagger divides text to sentences and tokens and annotates the tokens with a lemma (base form) and a Part-of-Speech (PoS) tag that are appropriate in the text.

The simple PoS tag is actually contained in a morpho-syntactic description of the token.
