How to cite Mylly?

Mylly is based on the Chipster technology developed at CSC – IT Center for Science. Please cite the original Chipster paper when you use Mylly in your publications:

M Aleksi Kallio, Jarno T Tuimala, Taavi Hupponen, Petri Klemelä, Massimiliano Gentile, Ilari Scheinin, Mikko Koski, Janne Käki, Eija I Korpelainen: Chipster: user-friendly analysis software for microarray and other high-throughput data (2011) BMC Genomics 12: 507

Note that references for the third party analysis tools integrated in Mylly are available in the tool manual pages (accessed by the "More help" button in Mylly). Please also cite these when appropriate.
